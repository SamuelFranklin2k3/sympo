About Us:

BLITZKRIEG is a prestigious national-level technical symposium hosted by the Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) department of RMK Engineering College. is event provides a platform for students from across the country to participate in a series of technical and non-technical events designed to enhance their knowledge and skills. Participants have the opportunity to engage in various competitions, workshops, and seminars, where they can showcase their talents, learn from experts, and network with peers. BLITZKRIEG aims to foster innovation, creativity, and technical excellence among students while providing a fun and competitive environment.
